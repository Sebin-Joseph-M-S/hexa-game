Getting Started

How do I start playing?

Sign up with a username and password, choose your faction and class, then start walking! The game will automatically detect your location and allow you to capture hexagons.

Do I need to download an app?

No! Hexa is a web-based game. Simply open it in your mobile browser and start playing. For the best experience, add it to your home screen.

What devices are supported?

Any device with a modern browser and GPS capability. Works best on smartphones with Chrome, Safari, or Firefox.

gps_fixed

GPS & Location

How accurate is the GPS tracking?

GPS accuracy typically ranges from 5-50 meters depending on your device, weather, and surroundings. Urban areas with tall buildings may have reduced accuracy.

Does GPS work indoors?

GPS signal is weaker indoors. For best results, play outdoors with a clear view of the sky.

Can I play while driving?

The game detects speeds above 30 km/h and switches to passive mode. You won't be able to capture hexes while driving for safety reasons.

Why does my location keep jumping?

This is normal GPS behavior. Make sure location services are enabled, you're outdoors, and your device has a clear view of the sky.

Battery & Performance

Does Hexa drain my battery?

Yes, continuous GPS tracking uses battery. We recommend playing with a power bank for long sessions. The Wake Lock feature keeps your screen on, which also consumes power.

How can I save battery while playing?

Lower screen brightness, close other apps, and use battery saver mode on your device. Consider bringing a portable charger for longer walks.

Can I play in the background?

No, web browsers stop GPS tracking when the app is in the background. Keep Hexa open and your screen on to continue playing.

Gameplay

How big is each hexagon?

Each hex is approximately 0.1 km² (174m edge length). You need to walk about 174 meters to enter a new hex.

What happens if I run out of energy?

You can't capture enemy hexes without energy. Walk through neutral or your own hexes to regenerate energy (+5 per hex). Visit your home base for free energy.

Can I change my faction or class?

Faction choice is permanent. Class changes may be available in future updates.

How do I defend my territory?

Revisit your hexes to increase defense level (max 5 stars). Higher defense makes it more expensive for enemies to capture.

Account & Privacy

Is my location data safe?

Yes. We only store hex captures, not your exact GPS coordinates. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can other players see my exact location?

No. Players only see which hexagons you've captured, not your real-time location.

The map isn't loading

Check your internet connection. Refresh the page. Clear browser cache and try again.

GPS location is not updating
