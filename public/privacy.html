gps_fixed

Location Privacy

Important: We Do NOT Store Exact GPS Coordinates

Hexa uses H3 hexagonal indexing. We only store which hexagons you've visited, not your precise latitude/longitude. Each hexagon covers ~0.1 km², providing location privacy while enabling gameplay.

  • • GPS data is processed locally in your browser
  • • Only H3 hex indexes are sent to our servers
  • • Other players cannot see your real-time location
  • • Location data is used solely for gameplay purposes

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • • Mapbox: Map rendering service (see Mapbox Privacy Policy)
  • • Railway: Database hosting service (see Railway Privacy Policy)
  • • Law Enforcement: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • • Public Leaderboards: Username and statistics only (no location data)

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • • Maintain your login session
  • • Remember dark mode preference
  • • Cache game data for better performance

You can clear local storage through your browser settings, but this will log you out.
